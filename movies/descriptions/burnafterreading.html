Burn After Reading

Genre(s): Comedy, Criminal
Directed By:Joel Coen, Ethan Coen
Cast:John Malkovich, George Clooney, Frances McDormand, Brad Pitt, Tilda Swinton, Richard Jenkins, J.K. Simmons, David Rasche

Release Date:12 September 2008

Intelligence is relative.

Ozzie Cox is a CIA veteran afraid of being fired. He puts his memoirs on disk and accidentally leaves it at a gym. Chad Feldheimer, who works at the gym, finds the disc and intends to blackmail Cox with his boss Linda Litzke. Harry Pfarrer who is assigned to recover the disc is also sleeping with Katie Cox, Ozzie's wife.
