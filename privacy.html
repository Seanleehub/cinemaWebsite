Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the services offered on the ame.com website operated by Advanced Motion Experience. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the Advanced Motion Experiences site. Usage of the Advanced Motion Experiences website is conditional upon you agreeing to be bound by this privacy policy.

1. Your personal information is important to us
Advanced Motion Experiences respects the importance of your privacy, and that you have a right to make your own choices about how your personal information is collected and used. We know that when you provide personal information to us you are showing your trust and we take that seriously. Unless you directly give us consent to do otherwise, Advanced Motion Experience will only collect and use your personal information as we explain below.

2. Collecting information about you
Except as stated in below, Advanced Motion Experiences will not collect or monitor any personal information about you without your consent:

we collect information when you enter your personal information on our site, for example by joining the AME Movie Club or taking part in a competition, when we ask for information such as your email address, name, address, date of birth and what movies you like the most;

if you register on the site, we may also collect information on how you use the site in order to make it as interesting as possible for you; and

if you contact us, we may also keep a record of that contact. We will only collect additional personal information, other than that explained here, with your consent.

3. Using and disclosing your personal information.
Our purpose in collecting information about you is to:
provide you with information which interests you and provide you with a better website experience with information and special offers that are more relevant to your interests. Advanced Motion Experiences will not use or disclose any information about you without your consent, unless required by law

we believe it necessary to fulfil the above purposes or otherwise to provide you with a service which you have requested to implement our terms and condition to protect the rights, property or personal safety of another Advanced Motion Experiences user, any member of the public or Advanced Motion Experiences.

In seeking to give you the most worthwhile experience of our website possible, Advanced Motion Experiences may use your personal information to send you advertisements about products and services which we think you are likely to be interested in. We do not disclose information about you to advertisers. Instead, advertisers give us advertisements and tell us the kind of audience they wish to reach. Advanced Motion Experiences then takes the advertisement and displays it to users who are most likely to have an interest in the product or service.

In order to operate the website, fulfil the purposes set out above or otherwise deliver a service we may sometimes share your personal information with a service partner or a company connected with Advanced Motion Experience (by, for example, a common shareholder or some common commercial interest). We will not otherwise disclose your personal information to other companies without your explicit consent.
